# Example brief — SaaS product

Paste after `/premium-site-factory:build-premium-site`:

```
Create a premium website for Ledgerline, a B2B SaaS that automates month-end close for
mid-size finance teams. Industry: SaaS/fintech. Audience: controllers and CFOs.
Primary CTA: book a demo (https://cal.example.com/ledgerline). Secondary: product tour.
Domain: ledgerline.example.com. Email: hello@ledgerline.example.com.
Style: light editorial, paper white with ink black, one signal-green accent, monospace
details for numbers and metrics. Confident, concrete, zero buzzwords.
Product pillars: automated reconciliation, close checklist, audit trail, ERP integrations.
Signature section idea: an annotated close-week timeline that compresses from 10 days
to 3 as you scroll.
Asset mode: no-api — typography and product UI mockups carry the design.
```

Expected result: product-led light-mode site with outcome-driven copy ("close in 3 days,
not 10"), a data-storytelling signature section, demo CTA (not WhatsApp), all metrics
placeholder-marked in `SITE_BRIEF.md`, build green, localhost running.
